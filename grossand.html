About Grossand

Let me tell you a little bit about myself...

Grøssand Boys are two young potential Artists/Producers/DJ’s who started in the music scene since December 2019. The two guys met each other in Juli 2017 at the biggest music event, TML.

On that moment, their friendship and magic journey started. Because of the social distance between both guys they decided to do some cultural exchange between Belgium and the Netherlands. Both were becoming closer because they shared the same values, vision and mission! Week after week, they travelled to almost every club and music event to gain some experience and knowledge.

After 3 years observing and raving in the house and techno scene, they decided to step up in the game to bring their own identity. Grøssand was born! Two different persons, opinions, backgrounds and identities are falling together. Both pieces combined, is what Grøssand stands for. Grøssand is the progressive techno duo coming forth from an unlikely tale of borderless friendship.

We aren’t only focused on our music but also on the connection with people. Every set, we try to tell a story and take the audience on a journey to experience! Because music is our passion, emotions, it is our Identity! Expect expressive melodic techno infused with dark and raw industrial undergound productions.

Both Boys also creating their own sound on the following names : @jordan_x_grossand @stijn_x_grossand_music
