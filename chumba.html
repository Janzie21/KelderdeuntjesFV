Chumba

About Chumba

Let me tell you a little bit about myself...

This dj/producer is becoming a household name in the local Belgium techno scene. “chumba” is known for the harder work in Techno and minimal music. The love for music caused him to start playing music.

At first it was mainly tech-house and minimal, but over the years techno has become the thread trough his sets. In the meantime he has already shared the decks with the top of the techno world, Amelie Lens, Dense & Pika , Joran Van Pol , Roberto capuano , Dj Rush , Fatimah haji.

Besides Being a Dj , “chumba” is also founder of techno concept “KELDERDEUNTJES”
