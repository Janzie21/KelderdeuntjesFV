Who are we

Kelderdeuntjes is a techno minimal concept that has been rocking the dance floor since 2016. The first baby steps were taken in the backstage room of the legendary club "La Rocca"!

It soon became clear that this concept was something special and we moved to the ballroom of la rocca where legendary evenings are etched in the memories of many techno lovers such as evenings with Joyhauser, Amelie lens, Dense & pika, Joran van pol and many more...

It became clear that we had to spread our wings and make our mark in the Belgian techno scene starting in Antwerp.

We had events in: De Spiegeltent , club vague, and beautiful collabs in zodiac with Antwerp techno & untz untz and one in the shop with B-rave!

Besides all that party violence, we also try to be a platform for upcoming DJ/producer within our capabilities. Since the far-reaching measures, we have started a 2-weekly podcast on soundcloud where national and international DJs fire a podcast of +/- one hour at us! We hope to see you back on our dance floor soon And don’t forget ravers….
