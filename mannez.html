Mannez

About Mannez

Let me tell you a little bit about myself...

Manezz is an electronic music artist: DJ, producer and drummer since a very young age. His 4-decks techno performance with live drums driving up the rhythmical session and generates a powerful danceable vibe. Manezz brings sets alive with his fine technical skills and by adding live instruments or samples.

His style is undefined special in the area of industrial spiritual techno. Feel the energy and connection when you are part of Manezz his story through music. Sharing Manezz's story does not end with DJ sets. To become fully independent in sharing the power of music, he wants to create his own tracks. Manezz is experiencing and defining his own sound and style. There is a lot more to come in bringing live aspects into his sets and tracks.

For more than 10 years, Manezz has been organizing and promoting techno events in Belgium and loves to throw nightlife events, live streams, festivals and other events around the electronic music culture. He is co-founder and resident artist of Full Spectrum with event concept 'Weerslag’, an industrial melodic techno event that connects with nature.
